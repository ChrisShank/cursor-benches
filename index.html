Welcome internet traveler!

This park is a place of refuge from the bustling internet.

This park was conceived around the provocation of how we can experience being able to sit next to someone on the internet. It weaves in ideas of public space, benches, and nature to provide refuge from an increasingly hostile, privatized, and lonely internet.

The ampitheater screens movies daily. Feel free to join by sitting on a mat!
